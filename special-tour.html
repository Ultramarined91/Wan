Geylang Food Tour
A taste of durians and exotic local delicacies


Tuesdays
Sundays


3 hours


From $70/pax

Bring your food journey away from the city with a trip down to Geylang, Singapore's hotbed for night suppers and gastronomic local delights. On this 3-hour guided tour, you'll walk the Geylang "Lorongs" (term for streets & alleyways) and sample different Singapore street food that are irresistible even for the locals.

Challenge your tastebuds with durians, famed as the "King of Fruits" in Southeast Asia along with other delicacies such as dim sum (bite-sized chinese tapas), turtle soups, frog leg porridge and much more. You won't know what you're missing out till you try this tour.
