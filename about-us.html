Our Story

It’s been a long time since our first tour in 1978. But some things remain the same. WAN focuses on small, hidden (sometimes subterranean) shops and restaurants that are frequented by locals. And we like to keep the groups small too – no larger that 16 people to ensure that YOU have a personal experience

The Lion Spirit

As one of Singapore's largest food tour operator, we explore new destinations and venture into opening the roads less travelled, we consistently review and revisit existing ones with new itineraries and experiences. We offer a diverse selection of tours, where there is something for everyone. We run the biggest free walking tours in the country. Expect only quality and authentic experience. Master-of-all-trades.

Our Guides

We employ a team of culinary enthusiasts, cultural experts, actors and comedians who are very smart and extremely proud to share their knowledge of the Little Red Dot with you. Our guides work exclusively for Little Lion Tours and many have been working with us since the start! They adore Lion City food & culture and can’t wait to share it with you.
