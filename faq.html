Yes, all our themed tours are on first-come-first-served basis, i.e. we only service 20 pax per themed tour per available tour date.

For example:
Our Michelin Food Tour is available on 29 & 31 Dec. If a group of 20 book the food tour on 29 Dec, the tour slot for 29 Dec would be closed immediately. Another group who is interested in the tour cannot book for 29 Dec but can still book for 31 Dec.
